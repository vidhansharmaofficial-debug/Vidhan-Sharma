About

The Builder Behind the Ideas

Vidhan Sharma is a student innovator, inventor, and technology builder from Jammu & Kashmir whose ideas consistently reach national stages — not because they're theoretical, but because they're built, demonstrated, and defended.

His philosophy is simple: don't just think about problems, build something. From the Zap Shoe — a kinetic energy harvesting wearable — to the Cliff Guard Car, every project starts with a real-world problem and ends with a functioning prototype.

Vidhan has represented his region at IIT Jammu, CSIR-NAL Bengaluru, CSIR-IIIM, and the National Startup Festival, pitching innovations directly to ministers, directors, and researchers.

Beyond hardware, he is a photographer, content creator, and entrepreneur-in-training — equally comfortable in a laboratory and behind a lens. His ambitions extend from patents and startups to UAV research and global competitions.

He is currently incubated at IIT Jammu, working on the next version of the Zap Shoe and exploring adjacent innovations in wearable technology and IoT.

"Build first, perfect later. Real-world problems don't wait for the perfect solution."
